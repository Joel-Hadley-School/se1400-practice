Classifying Video Games

  • Explorer
  • Achiever
  • Socializer
  • Killer

An Explorer tries to find everything about a games enviroment while also learning more about the games story.

Games this type might like:

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild
A very expansive open-world game with plently of things to find and see.
Outer Wilds
Gives an expansive adventure through space and the solar system.
Superliminal
A surreal, dreamlike puzzle game revolving around perspective and optical illusions.

An Achiever goes for any and all ahievements and quests that can be completed in the game, finding every possible secret they can.

Games this type might like:

Balatro
A fun singleplayer card game where you can unlock new cards through certain quests.
Super Meat Boy
A very tough platformer where getting achievements is just as hard as it is rewarding.
The Stanley Parable
Also a good exploration game, this game has multiple endings and changes that you can try to find!

A Socializer likes to play with friends or work together to either have fun or reach a common goal.

Games this type might like:

World of Warcraft
A massive online multiplayer game of fighting and questing.
Fortnite
A battle royale shooter where you can get together with your friends to try and get a win!
Lethal Company
A game where you must work together as a team to collect trash for quota, but monsters might be there to stop you...

A Killer is a ruthless player who fights and attacks enimies or other players as their favorite way to play.

Games this type might like:

Call of Duty
A shooter game experience where the only goal is to hold ground and kill everyone against you.
Among Us
A game where you have to kill the other players without being voted out.
Dark Souls
A tough action game with insane difficulty and rewarding kills
